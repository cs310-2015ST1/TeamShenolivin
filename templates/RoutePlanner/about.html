About MegaByke RoutePlanner

This app is for planning your bike route, with up to 5 locations!
Create an account, log in and click on the "Save Route" button to revisit the routes you've planned later, or to share on Facebook.
Click on the "Plot Routes" routes button see a part of the bike route for part for your trip, or on "Plot All Routes" to automatically include all 5 locations in your bike route.

This app is brought to you by Team Shenolivin.
